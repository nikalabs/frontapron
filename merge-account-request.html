Note

Business Account(s) is used to mimic your company's organization structure. Each business account can create many sub business accounts that are directly reporting to it. If a sub business account is a Store type, it can't create its sub business account layer anymore. Each business account can change its reporting relationship by using the Merge Accounts feature.

User Account(s) is created by each business account to help it manage Special postings. User accounts have limited access to the functions of the business account that created them.

Merge Account(s) is used to change the business account's reporting relationship either within the same company or with another company (M&A). The merged-to account will send a merge request by providing the merged-from business account's FrontApron Username and Account ID. After the merged-from business account receives the merge request and approves it, the merged-from business account (together with any business accounts under it) will become a sub business account of the merged-to account.
