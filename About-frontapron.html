About FrontApron

Company:

FrontApron empowers timely private sharing of all-you-want New, Fresh, and On-promotion product and service specials from all local businesses anytime and anywhere. And we give back 10% of annual revenue to local schools and charities chosen by our users.

Mission:

Founded in 2014, FrontApron’s mission is to give local people and businesses the power to share and make the local-commerce and local-communities more connected and participatory. People and businesses use FrontApron to privately stay connected and informed and to discover what is good for themselves and their local communities daily.

Team:

Inspired to empower local businesses to effectively engage local consumers and conserve resources, FrontApron’s determined and dedicated team has combined their many years of business, technology, and entrepreneurship experiences to help people and businesses anywhere in the world to establish their own local-commerce offline –to-online experiences however they want, one at a time. We are a group of innovators, family men and women, and community leaders. We aim to make our communities and the world a better place.
